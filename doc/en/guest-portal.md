# Guest portal

**Path:** Sidebar → **Guest Portal**

Guest-facing or staff-managed portal features for in-stay communication and requests.

## Purpose

Bridge between front desk and guests for:

- Service requests
- Messages
- Portal activity logging

## Access

Visible to **Admin**, **Manager**, and **Reception** roles.

## Change log

Guest portal actions appear in **Audit / change logs** under `guestportal` for traceability.

## Related modules

| Module | Connection |
|--------|------------|
| **Messages** | Staff/guest communication log |
| **Services** | Fulfillment of guest requests |
| **Bookings** | Active stay context |
